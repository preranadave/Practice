Strengthen Your Relationship to Plants through Traditional Aromatic Medicine Practices

Uncover the wisdom of plants by learning how to craft your own incense, perfume, and medicines from fragrant botanicals.

Since time immemorial, our ancestors have roasted resins, burned botanicals, and made medicine from the plants near their homes. When used wisely, these herbs have the power to lift the spirits, open the mind, and support the body’s natural healing ability.

Our one-of-a-kind online courses tap into that wisdom - combining traditional incense and perfume-making techniques with hands-on herbal and aromatherapy practices for making salves, oils, extracts, and other healing formulas.
