meet mariska, the heart behind kidsbarn

When I, Mariska Visser, turned 40 last summer (2023) I decided that the time had come to make the decision to start my own business. And to make that dream come true, I became the new owner of the successful kids concept store Kidsbarn on February 1, 2024.

After a career of 17 years at YoungCapital in recruitment, the last years ultimately responsible as Managing Director, that means learning a lot of new things and a leap into the deep as an entrepreneur. I am really looking forward to it!

In addition, I have been trained as an interior stylist and am in training for interior designer. I use this professional knowledge to be able to make the best purchases, but also to advise on the furnishing of the most beautiful children's rooms.
